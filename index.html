Introduction

The sole reason for the existence of this blog is to relieve my self of inner stress which has been building up for a long time.

Who knows if it will help at all, or what this might lead to.

One of my hopes is that someone, in a deep dark place, at some point, somehow discovers this website and can get motivation out of it, and maybe, feel less alone.

Because I believe, as cruel as it might sound. Seeing others suffer, especially in a similar way to one self, it can make you feel better, and have another perspective. It's almost like therapy.

About me

I'm a 23 year old student, that somehow feels like I stopped mentally aging after 17, and I'm stuck.

A lot has happened in my life in the past couple of years, much bad, but also much good.

I've had the pleasure of knowing what being near death feels like, and what being in love feels like, like really deeply in love, soulmate kind of thing.

And that crazy part is that I was able to experience these two totally different perspectives of ones lifetime within the same year, heck, the same month...

As I've been thinking about these supposed parallels in life I've rather come to discover that they're not so different from eachother. Both bring a tremendous love for life and suffering, sometimes at the same time...
